General Information

Neighar is an application that facilitates connecting with individuals who share similar interests and personalities. Through the app, users can engage in activities such as dating, chatting, making friends, and meeting new people. The app is available for free download on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

Neighar operates in three simple steps. Firstly, users are required to download the free iOS or Android app and take a quick 5-question test to determine their unique 16 personality type. Secondly, the app recommends personalities that are likely to be compatible and a good match based on the test results. Users are encouraged to be themselves and simply connect with those suggested personalities. Finally, users can choose to either like or pass on recommended personalities, and enjoy the process of connecting with like-minded individuals.

Neighar is completely free to download. It offers all its basic features completely free of charge. Users can like or pass on people, as well as send messages and chat with other like-minded friends/dates without any cost.
